Edit distance

  1. Let \( X=x_1x_2\dots x_m \) and \( Y=y_1y_2\dots y_n \)
  2. Let \( E_{ij} \) denote the edit distance between \( A \)(new) and \( B \)(old) , \( 1\leq i\leq m \) , \( 1\leq i\leq n \)。
  3. Oberve \( x_i \) and \( y_j \) after alignment:

    Insertion
    \( E_{ij}=E_{i-1,j}+1 \)
    \( x_i \)
    -
    Deletion
    \( E_{ij}=E_{i,j-1}+1 \)
    -
    \( y_j \)
    Difference
    \( E_{ij}=E_{i-1,j-1}+diff(i,j) \)
    \( x_i \)
    \( y_j \)

\[ E_{00}=0 \] \[ \{ E_{0j}=j\;|\;\text{for }1\leq j\leq n\} \] \[ \{E_{i0}=i \;|\; \text{for }1\leq i\leq m \} \] \[ \{\; E_{ij}= min\{ \underline{E_{i-1,j}+1},\; \underline{E_{i,j-1}+1},\; \underline{E_{i-1,j-1}+diff(i,j)}, \} \; |\;\text{for }1\leq i\leq n, 1\leq j\leq m \; \} \] \[ diff(i,j) = \begin{cases} 0 \text{, if }x_i=y_j\\ 1 \text{, if }x_i\neq y_j\\ \end{cases} \]

Example

Old string(B): POLYNOMIAL
